# Supporter Conversion and Retention Research

## Purpose

Research notes for Prism Music/Career on converting casual listeners into paying supporters and retaining them without generic community-building advice.

## Core findings

Supporter conversion is usually driven by context, timing, and clear asks rather than vague audience-building.

- Warm moments matter: after a show, around a release, after a meaningful email/story, or when a fan has just engaged.
- Low-friction entry tiers are usually better than one expensive tier.
- Exclusive content, early access, demos, behind-the-scenes posts, livestreams, and tangible bonuses are common supporter incentives.
- A free-to-paid pathway can work better than a cold direct ask.
- Urgency helps only when genuine: limited items, time-bound campaigns, or specific goals.
- Retention is often more important than raw acquisition for small creators.
- Churn is not always personal: financial constraints, platform friction, unmet expectations, one-off benefit completion, and creator inactivity all matter.

## Practical implications for Dave

Music/Career should help Dave see and maintain a small supporter layer without turning it into a high-pressure creator economy machine.

Potential useful views:

- warm moments worth a direct ask;
- recent supporter-facing activity;
- draft supporter updates from existing work;
- lapsed/quiet periods without shame language;
- simple benefit promises Dave can actually sustain;
- manual notes on what supporters responded to.

## Product implications

Supporter data does not currently have a clear canonical owner.

Possible future owners:

1. EPK/Music-Career, if supporter state is treated as part of career/business operations.
2. Focus, if tracking is mostly routine/journal/task cadence.
3. Deferred/manual, until a stable pattern emerges.

Current Beam recommendation: defer canonical schema/owner until real usage clarifies the natural home.

## Design cautions

- Do not build churn dashboards that amplify shame or panic.
- Do not overpromise monthly rewards Dave cannot sustain.
- Do not equate supporter retention with constant posting.
- Do not scrape or store platform data without an explicit safe read contract.
- Do not auto-message supporters.
- Do not expose supporter data on public EPK outputs.

## Beam synthesis

Supporter conversion/retention belongs to the broader Music/Career domain, not to a static EPK page. But canonical state ownership remains unresolved and should be reviewed before implementation.
